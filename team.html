Our Mission

Founded in 2017, WandX is a protocol for ‘basket trade’ and a decentralized trading platform for crypto token holders. Our mission is to create a one-stop, simple and smart digital asset management ecosystem that helps crypto asset holders manage their risks better, without fear or driven by hype.

The WandX Vision

Following an explosion of interest in blockchain projects and cryptocurrencies, investments in these new digital assets have become tricky, complicated and heavily dependent on market forces, including obscure indexes and centralized order books.


We are betting on the original methodologies of modern financial instruments that help in making trading and investing in crypto assets and crypto derivative products easy and prudent.


Apart from helping to align investments in digital assets with personal goals more closely, our vision is to make people feel confident about investing in blockchain-based digital assets and also managing these digital assets on their own.
