Complete Trading Platform

The WandX Desktop Application allows you to store, trade and manage assets on both NEO and Ethereum blockchains within one application. No need to transfer your tokens or upload your keys in order to complete trades.

Local ETH & NEO Wallets

The multi-chain desktop wallet allows you to create or connect Ethereum and NEO wallets to the app. This way, you safely and securely store keys on your local computer, sign transactions faster and manage funds better.

Multi-Blockchain DEX

Trade on a decentralized exchange on either Ethereum or NEO Blockchain on a single screen. WAND on Ethereum is used to obtain discounts on transaction fees, and WANDNEO is a staking Token used to obtain a percentage of trade fees on the NEO based DEX.

Multi-Chain Baskets

Store and trade ETH and NEO assets through our multi-chain basket protocol. Baskets provide an easily managed and cost-effective means of building a strong cryptocurrency portfolio and also help potentially reduce volatility by balancing out many of the risks associated with individual token purchases.
