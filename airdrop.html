Airdrop Instructions

Please follow the below steps to complete the WAND/WANDNEO 1:1 airdrop.

1. Download the WandX Desktop Application below and create a new NEO public address within it.

2. Enter the Ethereum address submitted during the Airdrop registration where your WAND is kept.

3. Enter thenew NEO address from the WandX desktop application into the form to the left.

4. Complete the airdrop by signing the transaction using either Metamask or Ledger.

5. You will receive the 1:1 airdrop into the NEO address which you created on thedesktop app on October 10th*.

* Airdrop distribution date subject to change based on circumstances beyond our control.
