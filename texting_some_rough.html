Multi blockchain desktop app

The current version of WandX desktop app is a one-stop decentralized token trading platform for both Ethereum (ERC20) and NEO (NEP-5) based tokens.

WandX Basket Protocol

Buy and sell a portfolio of ERC20 or NEP5 tokens in a single transaction by opting for themed token baskets created on smart contracts using the Basket Protocol.

Hybrid decentralized exchange ecosystem

Trade on an Ethereum or a NEO based decentralized exchange on the WandX desktop app without the hassle of signing transactions with an external wallet.

Ethereum and NEO wallet

Take advantage of the WandX desktop app to create a multi coin wallet or plug an existing wallet linked to Ethereum or NEO to trade and privately manage tokens. WandX doesn’t hold custody of Tokens, and all transactions go through the ETH or NEO Blockchains.

What is WandX?

WandX is the world’s first multi blockchain decentralized token exchange ecosystem
with a unique Basket Protocol that supports bulk token trading through smart contracts.

Why WandX?


Decentralized Trade

Trade Tokens on Ethereum or NEO while always holding control of your Tokens; Orders are hosted on the Blockchain; trades happen through the Blockchain, hence no central party confirms your transactions.

Decentralized Hedging

Hedge risks associated with volatile prices through purchasing a portfolio of Tokens with a single click. Purchase a "Decentralized Exchange Token" or "Stable Coin Basket" with a single click.

User owned custody

Create/import wallets on either Ethereum or NEO while always having control of your wallets.
